# 11 Source Policy

## Source Scope

The initial source scope is Scientific Spaces blog content.

## Source Requirements

- Preserve title.
- Preserve URL.
- Preserve date when available.
- Preserve category when available.
- Preserve images and references.
- Preserve citations and source links.

## AI Grounding Policy

AI answers must cite source material when using article or paper content.

## Bootstrap Constraint

No source ingestion is implemented in Bootstrap.
